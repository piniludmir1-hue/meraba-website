MERABA Brand Brief
==================

Positioning
-----------
Global Food Service Supply Solutions.

Core Tagline
------------
The simplicity of a local supplier.
The power of global sourcing.

What MERABA does
-----------------
MERABA sources, imports, purchases and supplies industrial food service products for catering operations, food manufacturers, hospitality groups and airlines.

Role
----
MERABA is not only a sourcing agent and not only a catalog seller. MERABA acts as a reliable supply partner: we buy, manage and deliver the products to the customer.

Key Message
-----------
One partner for sourcing, procurement, import, logistics and supply.

Target Customers
----------------
- Catering factories
- Food manufacturers
- Hospitality groups
- Institutional food service
- Airlines
- Procurement managers

Website Feeling
---------------
Premium, clean, industrial, international, reliable, minimal, aviation-grade.

Avoid
-----
- Cheap catalog look
- Marketplace look
- Simple packaging company look

Brand Accent
------------
Use the MERABA blue from the logo as the main accent color across CTAs, links and small graphical elements. If the exact logo color is not available, use a refined deep blue as the primary accent for a premium, industrial look.

Notes
-----
Keep typography strong and spacious; prioritize large imagery, clear hierarchy, and restrained use of accent color to communicate premium industrial quality.
