**Quiet confidence.**
A company that knows what it does exceptionally well and doesn't need to shout about it.

**Premium industrial.**
High-end materials and finishes with authentic operational credibility.

**Global but practical.**
International reach combined with pragmatic, no-nonsense execution.

**Not AI-generated.**
Avoid:
- Thin decorative divider lines
- Overly minimal empty sections
- Generic startup spacing
- Soft SaaS-style layouts
- Artificial symmetry everywhere
- Fake modern AI aesthetics

**Not startup SaaS.**
Avoid:
- Trendy micro-interactions
- Overly playful copy
- Generic gradient backgrounds
- Soft, rounded corners everywhere
- Engagement-baiting CTAs

**Not cheap catalog.**
Avoid:
- Product grid dumps
- Marketplace layouts
- Bargain-bin aesthetics
- Low-quality imagery
- Cluttered information hierarchies

## Core Design Principles

### Typography
- Use **strong, confident typefaces** (semibold/bold for headings)
- Maintain **large visual hierarchy** (hero headings should be powerful)
- **Plenty of breathing room** between text blocks
- **Minimal ornamental fonts** — function over decoration
- Clear contrast between body and display text

### Layout & Spacing
- **Large visual sections** with substantial padding
- **Realistic section proportions** (not overly compressed)
- **Strong block-based structure** (not scattered floating elements)
- **Mix of white and dark industrial sections** for depth
- **Avoid thin divider lines** — let sections breathe through color/whitespace
- **Intentional use of whitespace** (not filler)

### Color
- **White as dominant background** (#FFFFFF)
- **MERABA blue as accent** (#0f4c81) for CTAs, highlights, key elements
- **Dark charcoal/black** for operational, premium sections
- **Subtle grays** for secondary information
- **Restrained use of color** — let the content breathe

### Imagery
- **Large visual areas** (hero images, section backgrounds)
- **Industrial, real-world imagery** (supply chain, food service, logistics, manufacturing)
- **Professional photography** (no stock AI imagery)
- **Authentic production environments** (factories, kitchens, distribution centers)
- **People only when relevant** (procurement teams, operations, not smiling crowds)
- **Placeholder approach**: Use high-quality industrial stock initially; replace with real MERABA assets later

### Cards & Components
- **Subtle shadows and borders** (not heavy drop shadows)
- **Clean geometric shapes** (mostly rectangular, minimal decoration)
- **Generous internal padding** (cards should feel spacious)
- **Left accent borders** (small MERABA blue accent for key content)
- **Minimal rounded corners** (if any, only slightly rounded)

### Navigation & CTAs
- **Bold, uppercase navigation** (confident, clear hierarchy)
- **Substantial button sizes** (not tiny, clickable, confident)
- **Strong color contrast** for interactive elements
- **Hover states that are subtle** (opacity changes, not color shifts)
- **Clear visual hierarchy** between primary and secondary CTAs

## Page Structure Guidelines

### Hero Sections
- **Full-height or tall viewport** (not compressed)
- **Large, confident headings** (80px+ on desktop)
- **Substantial supporting copy** (not one-liners)
- **Strong CTA buttons** (prominent, not subtle)
- **Optional background imagery** (industrial, professional)
- **Multi-line taglines allowed** (add breathing room)

### Content Sections
- **Minimum 80px padding** (top/bottom)
- **Maximum 3-4 content blocks per section** (not grid dumps)
- **Strong section headings** (text-display or text-heading)
- **Introductory copy before content** (context and purpose)
- **Large visual spacing** between sections

### Lists & Features
- **Avoid bullet points** (use structured blocks instead)
- **Icon + heading + description format** (organized, scannable)
- **Generous vertical spacing** between items
- **Accent icons** (use MERABA blue when appropriate)
- **2-column layouts max** (not crowded grids)

### Forms & Inputs
- **Large input fields** (minimum 16px text size)
- **Clear labels and placeholders**
- **Substantial spacing between fields**
- **Strong, confident submit buttons**
- **Subtle field borders** (gray, not black)

## Interactive Elements

### Hover States
- **Opacity changes** (primary interaction feedback)
- **Subtle color shifts** (secondary elements)
- **No dramatic animations** (keep it professional)
- **Fast transitions** (300ms or less)

### Transitions
- **Smooth, eased** (ease-out preferred)
- **Purposeful** (not gratuitous)
- **Consistent timing** (300ms standard)
- **Accessibility-first** (respect prefers-reduced-motion)

## Responsive Design

### Mobile-First Approach
- **Maintain strong typography** even on small screens
- **Large touch targets** for interactive elements
- **Generous padding on mobile** (don't compress spacing)
- **Readable line lengths** (not stretched across full width)
- **Stack sections vertically** (clear, uncluttered)

### Breakpoints
- Mobile: 320px - 767px
- Tablet: 768px - 1023px
- Desktop: 1024px+

## Brand Voice in Design

Every design decision should communicate:
- **Reliability**: Solid structure, no gimmicks
- **Professionalism**: Clean, sophisticated execution
- **Scale**: Global reach, operational capability
- **Expertise**: Deep knowledge in food service supply
- **Confidence**: No need to over-explain or over-design

The design should feel like it was created by a **real high-end B2B design studio**, not AI or template-based tools.
